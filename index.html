Questions

Frequently asked

How much does it cost?

A one-time ₹3,000 / $50 for us to set everything up on your own Cloudflare account. There are no subscriptions and no monthly fees from us. After setup it runs on Cloudflare’s free tier, so your ongoing cost is $0.

Why is there a fee if Cloudflare is free?

You’re paying for the done-for-you setup, configuration and support — not the hosting. We provision the database and storage, secure the instance, wire up scheduled backups and automatic updates, and help you connect your apps. Cloudflare’s free tier covers the actual running cost.

Do I need a credit card?

No. We set Keynest up in KV storage mode, which doesn’t require a card on your Cloudflare account. (If you later want larger file attachments you can switch on Cloudflare R2, which does ask for a card — but it’s optional.)

Where is my data stored?

Entirely on your Cloudflare account, in the region you choose. Your vault is end-to-end encrypted with your master password, so even Cloudflare can’t read it — and we never see it at all.

Which apps can I use?

The official Bitwarden apps: browser extensions (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari, Brave…), iOS, Android, desktop and the CLI. There’s no separate app to install — you just set the server URL to your Keynest instance.

How do updates work?

Your instance checks for new releases automatically and redeploys itself — fully branded as Keynest — without touching your stored data. We set this up during your installation.

What happens if I want to leave?

It’s your account and your data. Export your vault any time from the app, and delete the Cloudflare resources whenever you like. No lock-in.

How do I pay?

Send us a setup request by email and we’ll reply with payment details — a one-time ₹3,000 (India) or $50 (international). Once payment is confirmed we schedule your setup, usually within a day.
